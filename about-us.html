About Dr. Nceba Hene

Internationally recognized expert dedicated to unlocking peak performance and driving positive behavioural change across individuals and organisations. Performance Scientist    |    Behavioural Strategist    |    Global Speaker
Work with Dr. Hene Read Key Shift
Dr. Nceba Hene is an internationally recognised expert, speaker, and executive dedicated to unlocking peak performance and driving positive behavioural change across individuals and organisations. His work is built on a rare combination of expertise spanning Corporate Wellness, Loyalty and Rewards, and High-Performance Sport.

With over 20 years of distinguished experience, Dr. Hene holds a PhD in Biokinetics and has earned prestigious accolades, including the ASAIPA National Medical Awards for Health Care Professional Excellence and the South African Loyalty Industry Talent of the Year.

His approach blends human psychology, performance science, and real-world high-pressure experience enabling him to design systems that drive measurable, sustained growth for teams and organisations.

From High-Performance Fields to Executive Boardrooms
Elite Sport Foundation

Dr. Nceba Hene began his career in elite sport, serving as fitness coach to South Africa's national women's rugby teams and contributing to their bronze medal win at the 2009 IRB Sevens Rugby World Cup.

Business Leadership

He later applied performance science to business, holding senior leadership roles across health, banking, insurance, and fuel. His work in wellness-driven loyalty programmes has earned both national and international recognition.

His unique perspective comes from operating at the intersection of three worlds: elite sport, corporate leadership, and academic research.
