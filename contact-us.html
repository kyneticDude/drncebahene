Frequently Asked Questions
How far in advance should I book Dr. Hene?

For optimal availability, we recommend booking 3-6 months in advance. However, Dr. Hene can sometimes accommodate shorter timelines depending on his schedule.

Can keynotes be customized for my organization?

Absolutely. Every keynote is tailored to your audience, industry, and specific challenges. We conduct pre-event consultations to ensure maximum relevance and impact.

Does Dr. Hene speak internationally?

Yes. Dr. Hene is available for speaking engagements globally and has presented in multiple countries across different continents.

What's included in a keynote booking?

Standard bookings include the keynote presentation, pre-event consultation, customized content, Q&A session (if requested), and post-event follow-up materials.

How can I order KEY SHIFT in bulk?

For bulk orders (10+ copies), please use the contact form above and select "Book Order (Bulk)". We offer special pricing and can arrange signed copies for your team or event.
